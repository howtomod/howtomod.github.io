Change colors
  1. Goto colors.css and google color picker
  2. most of them are in hsl, so pick your color and copy the *HSL* value
  3. put hsl value in the () and remove the degree symbol
  4. Your done!
Notes: Do not change the last value in the four digit, for example (70,50,80,*50*)
